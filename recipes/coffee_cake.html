Sour Cream Coffee Cake

Description: A picture of a tube pan (source: America's Test Kitchen)

Ingredients:

  • Batter:
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 1 cup stick margarine or butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 cup sour cream (plain yogurt also works)
    • 2 cups flour
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Topping:
    • 4 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 1 cup chopped pecans

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Cream sugar and margarine together. Add vanilla and eggs. Add sour cream. Add flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix sugar, cinnamon, and pecans.
  4. Pour half of batter into a greased and floured tube pan. Sprinkle half of the topping over batter. Add remaining batter, and sprinkle on rest of the topping.
  5. Bake for 1 hour. Cake should spring back when lightly pressed.
